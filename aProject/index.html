Welcome to Tara's Society

Who we are

The Tara Society is a social, interactive group, with the same goal as the local small business owner, to grow the business (specialized in gastronomy and basic services of this nature such as groceries). We are committed to giving a helping hand to new entrepreneurs and micro merchants to improve and grow the individual autonomy of their businesses, through connections, advice, quality raw materials and reasonable prices; taking the role of intermediary to establish having at hand the benefits that large companies enjoy.

How we work?

Microenterprises are usually small local stores, with limited budget and services; They go through difficulties that an entrepreneur is not prepared to face. Our work works because we face the financial world through the phrase "unity is strength", the strength of higher-ranking companies achieves; Being a society we can access benefits with suppliers, creditors and clients that as a micro business we will not be able to take advantage of. For example, three bakeries require sugar, however their sugar orders are minimal, so they cost the same as in a commercial store; When those three bakeries join together you have access to our shopping list; where they can purchase their sugar orders at wholesale prices from the factory; since we place an order on behalf of the company, and we distribute only what you ordered without the additional commercial price. These and more benefits await you.
