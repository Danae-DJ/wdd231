Purpose

The Tara Society is a social, interactive group, with the same goal as the local small business owner, to grow the business (specialized in gastronomy and basic services of this nature such as groceries). We are committed to giving a helping hand to new entrepreneurs and micro merchants to improve and grow the individual autonomy of their businesses, through connections, advice, quality raw materials and reasonable prices; taking the role of intermediary to establish having at hand the benefits that large companies enjoy.
