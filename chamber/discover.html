Discover Idaho

While you think about a better future, you should also relax!!!

Lava Hot Springs

430 East Main Street, Lava Hot Springs, ID 83246

Lava Hot Spring are natural hot springs with facilities operated by the state. The swimming pools provide safety and hygiene measures; They are also enjoyable due to the convenient variety between the pools, such as shallow and shallow places, monitored temperature to enjoy the water at different temperatures; maximizing relaxation.

Idaho Botanical Garden

2355 Old Penitentiary Rd, Boise, ID 83712

The Idaho Botanical Garden is located next to the former prison in Boise, Idaho. This attraction is a beautiful landscape focused on delighting through an exorbitant number of species from the plant kingdom. The tour through the botany allows you to feel like you are on an environmental adventure. There are places to sit and relax freely. There is also a Koi fish pond.

Payette Lake

Payette Lake, a 5330 acre lake in the mountains of McCall, Idaho.

Payette Lake is located in McCall, Idaho. It is a beautiful lake with Ponderosa State Park running through the middle, with great hiking and beautiful scenery, ideal for clear days. There are several restaurants on the lake with excellent views and a beautiful beach which you will undoubtedly want to enjoy. McCall offers many enjoyable day hikes to local lakes and waterfalls, as well as other enjoyable outdoor activities.

City Of Rocks National Reserve

3035 Elba Almo Rd, Almo, ID 83312-9700

City Of Rocks National Reserve is located just outside the National Reserve in the community of Almo. There are services are available in the area, such as fuel, groceries, camping supplies, restaurants, lodging, post office, and hot springs. Don't worries we have fantastics atention for you.

Morrison-Knudsen Nature Center

600 S Walnut St, Boise, ID 83712-7729

As the name says, we are talking about a nature center where you can walk and enjoy the large pond with small creatures such as: fish, butterflies, bees and wasps enjoying the flowers in the garden while you learn the history of the various species of insects or fish. Parking and the center are free.

Schweitzer

10000 Schweitzer Mountain Rd, Sandpoint, ID 83864-9235

For outdoor winter activities this is the right place; Schweitzer is a beautiful place, a good place to ski, you will find good snow, good slopes and incredible views. They have a beautiful lake, good restaurants; In addition, the town offers you an important cultural history.

Yellowstone Bear World

6010 S 4300 W, Rexburg, ID 83440-4405

Considering that one goes to Yellowstone wanting to see bears, this local refuge is the best option to see them up close. It is an attraction for children and adults where you can touch animals and learn. There are also mountain goats, deer, among other beautiful animals that walk freely and that you could feed. The place is excellent a great attraction of Idaho Falls.

Lake Pend Oreille

365 North Triangle Drive Ponderay, Idaho 83852

Lake Pend Oreille is a truly beautiful lake. Ideal for boating, swimming, fishing and kayaking. With facilities that allow the adventurer to engage in activities. The area is surrounded by mountains and forests and water is easily accessible. The beach near Sam Owens State Park has a sand volleyball court and kayak rentals, plus kiosks with basic amenities, all so you can stay worry-free all day.
